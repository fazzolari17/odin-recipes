Black Beans

Ingredients

  • 1 Can of Black Beans, undrained
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Packet Sazón without Annatto
  • 1 tbsp Oregano Leaf
  • 2 tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 1/2 cup chopped Onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped Bellpepper
  • 1 Jalapeño pepper chopped
  • 2 cloves of Garlic chopped

Directions

  1. Add onions, peppers, garlic and olive oil to a saucepan and sauté until fragrant and soft. About 5-8 minutes over med-hi heat
  2. Once vegetables are soft add water, apple cider vinegar, black beans, sazon and oregano. Bring to a boil and lower heat until cooked through and flavors have blended. Approximately 20 min
  3. While the black beans are simmering make rice according to package. If desired.
